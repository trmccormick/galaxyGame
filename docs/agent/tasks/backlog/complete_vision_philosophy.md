### **Rejection of EVE's PvP-Centric Model**
**EVE Online**: A universe defined by conflict, where CCP celebrates "ships destroyed per hour" as their primary success metric.

**Galaxy Game**: A universe defined by creation, where success is measured by:
- **Colonies established** across new worlds
- **Systems explored** and understood
- **Trade networks built** connecting humanity
- **Technologies advanced** for peaceful progress
- **Cooperative projects** uniting players

### **Humanity's Realistic Future**
Not endless corporate warfare destroying each other's infrastructure, but collaborative expansion into the stars - building new homes, establishing sustainable trade routes, and advancing scientific understanding for the benefit of all.
