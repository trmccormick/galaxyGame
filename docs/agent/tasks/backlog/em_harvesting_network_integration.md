# EM Harvesting Network Integration

## Task Overview
Implement the Exotic Matter (EM) harvesting and distribution network where natural wormholes become power sources for artificial wormhole stations, creating a galactic energy economy.

## Background
Natural wormholes release EM when they collapse or "snap." This energy can be harvested to power artificial wormhole stations, creating a symbiotic relationship between natural and artificial wormhole technologies.

**Stabilization Satellites:**
- **Harvest Mode**: Siphon EM from collapsed natural wormholes
- **Logistics Mode**: Receive and distribute EM from Sol
- **Storage Capacity**: EM vault construction and management

**EM Generation Sources:**
- Natural wormhole collapses ("Hot Starts")
- Spatial tension from mass transit
- Residual blooms from "Snaps"
- Artificial generation through AWS operations
