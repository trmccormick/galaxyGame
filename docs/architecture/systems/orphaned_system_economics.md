# Orphaned System Economics (The Eden Scarcity Model)

## 1. Market Decoupling
When a system's `connectivity_status` is `orphaned`, the local market must decouple from the Sol-side price index.

### 1.1 Local Scarcity Pricing
* **Mechanism:** Prices for Tier III components (Advanced Computers, Quantum Arrays) increase exponentially based on local inventory levels rather than "Galactic" averages.
* **The "Build" Incentive:** High market prices for I-Beams and Panels should trigger the AI Manager to prioritize local **Refinery & Factory** construction over attempting to "Buy" from a non-existent import market.

## 2. Resource Hoarding & Allocation
The AI Manager must shift from "Profit Optimization" to "Project Completion."

* **Reserved Inventory:** Materials tagged for the **AWS Anchor** (e.g., the 5,000 I-Beams needed for bracing) are removed from the public buy/sell orders to prevent players from "draining" the construction supply.
* **Strategic Credit:** During the orphaned period, the AI may issue "Eden-Only" scrip/credits to players who contribute raw ore to the AWS project, redeemable for high-tier gear once the connection is restored.

## 3. The Reconnection "Flash"
Upon successful **Wormhole Injection**, the local market re-syncs. 
* **Impact:** Sudden price correction. If Eden I-Beams were 10x Sol price, the opening of the gate should cause a "Gold Rush" of traders moving mass from Sol to Eden to capture the arbitrage.
