## Three-Tier Infrastructure (Jupiter Pattern + Titan Surface)

**Tier 1: Orbital Station (Phase 5)**
- Ship construction and repair facilities
- Crew habitation (15 personnel)
- Manufacturing for spacecraft components
- Research laboratories for cryogenic technology

**Tier 2: Orbital Depot (Phase 2)**
- Gas processing from atmospheric harvesters
- Automated refueling station for harvester docking cycle
- Cargo transfer and buffer storage
- Quality control systems

**Tier 3: Moon Network (Phases 8-11)**
- **Enceladus**: Plume harvesting (water/organics) - 3 crew
- **Dione**: Ice mining (oxygen/water production) - 4 crew
- **Rhea**: Multi-purpose emergency depot - 3 crew
- **Network Coordination**: Automated inventory management

**Unique to Saturn: Titan Surface (Phase 4)**
- Large-scale methane processing
- Chemical manufacturing plants
- Fuel storage and distribution (20 crew)
- Provides resources for orbital station construction
