# 3D-Printed Fabricators (Mk1–Mk3)

This document describes the three generations of 3D-printed fabricators foundational to Luna and planetary ISRU construction.

## Mk1: 3D-Printed Fabricator Mk1
- Entry-level, ruggedized for day-one deployment
- Consumes qualified regolith feedstock
- Produces panels, ibeams, and basic structural elements
