# Intent: L1 Depot Operations & Gas Processing

## 1. Core Identity
The L1 Depot is a **Hybrid Structure**: a high-volume storage depot combined with a heavy-duty **Atmospheric Refinery**. It is the primary node for the Venus-Titan-Luna logistics triangle.

## 2. The Feedstock Loop
The Depot is designed to process raw atmospheric imports into high-grade propellants:
- **Venusian Input**: CO2 (Carbon Dioxide). 
- **Processing**: Sabatier/Electrolysis conversion into **LOX (Liquid Oxygen)**.
- **Titan Input**: CH4 (Methane).
- **Storage**: Cryogenic Methane is stored as the primary fuel half of the mix.

## 3. Skimmer Operations & Redundancy
- **Primary Docking**: Skimmers (Venus/Titan) prioritize L1 Depot docking for immediate gas transfer and automated refueling.
- **Secondary Landing (Luna)**: If L1 is at capacity or offline, skimmers land at the Luna Settlement. 
- **The "Efficiency Gap"**: Luna processing capacity is significantly lower than L1. Landing at Luna is a contingency that results in slower turnaround times and higher fuel overhead.

## 4. Resource Distribution
- **Refined Surplus**: Excess refined gases (O2, N2, noble gases) are shuttered down to the Luna Settlement or held for Earth-bound resupply missions.
- **Construction Source**: Bulk structures (I-Beams/Panels) are sourced from Luna; high-complexity Avionics/Electronics are Tier 3 Earth imports.
